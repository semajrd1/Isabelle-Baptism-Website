On a warm, serene day in Crete,
By the turquoise, sun-kissed sea,
Loved ones gather 'round to meet,
Isabelle baptized and filled with glee.

Her parents hold her with tender care,
In a gown of pure, angelic white,
As the priest blesses her with a prayer,
And bestows upon her heaven's light.

Afterwards, a feast of Greek delights,
Olives, feta, and baklava too,
All savored under the warm sunlight,
As Isabelle begins her journey anew.
